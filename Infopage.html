Information

Types of anxiety:

  • Social anxiety - the fear of social situations
  • GAD - extreme worrying
  • OCD - repetitive behaviors

Symptoms of anxiety include:

  • Muscle tension
  • Headaches
  • Insomnia
  • Loss of appetite
  • Panic attacks
  • Nausea
  • Increase in heart rate/ breathing
